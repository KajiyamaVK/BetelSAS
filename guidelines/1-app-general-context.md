## 1. Overview

**Betel** is a mobile application designed for **children's religious education** (Christian Catechism). It focuses on teaching theological concepts through an engaging, interactive interface tailored for kids. The app combines lessons, music, and memorization tools to make learning accessible and fun.

### Core Purpose

To help children learn and memorize catechism questions and answers, understanding God and biblical principles through multimedia content.

### Target Audience

- **Primary**: Children (evident from the playful UI, simple language, and gamified elements).
- **Secondary**: Parents/Teachers (who might guide the learning or track progress).
